Welcome to my personal website! I am a PhD Candidate in Human-Computer Interaction and Computer Science at the University of Calgary, working with Dr. Anthony Tang (University of Toronto) in the RICELab group and in the Interactions Lab. I am also working with Dr. Carman Neustaedter in the Connections Lab at the School of Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University.

I have training and skills as a computer scientist, developer, designer, researcher, and communicator. My interests are in designing, building, and studying technologies that connect people across distances for work and play. For fun, I enjoy hiking, cycling, travelling, photography, dogs, cats, occasional video gaming, making up bad puns, and spending time with family and friends.
