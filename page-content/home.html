Welcome to my personal website! I am a PhD Candidate in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Computer Science at the University of Calgary, working with Dr. Anthony Tang (University of Toronto) in the RICELab group and in the Interactions Lab. I am also working with Dr. Carman Neustaedter in the Connections Lab at the School of Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University.

I work on technologies that bring people together. My research involves building the future of telepresence, remote collaboration, and social computing. I design, prototype, and evaluate user experiences for these purposes, involving emerging technologies such as virtual, augmented, and mixed reality (VR/AR/MR), telepresence robots, tangible and haptic user interfaces, drones, and video communication.
